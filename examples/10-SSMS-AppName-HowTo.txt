SSMS Application Name (PROGRAM_NAME) setup

1) In SSMS, click "Connect" -> "Database Engine" -> "Options >>"
2) Go to the "Additional Connection Parameters" tab.
3) Enter:
   Application Name=KXGT-yourname
4) Click "Connect".

Ensure that dba.Developer.AppName holds the exact same value for your developer row.
Your DDL trigger will then be able to resolve the developer without SESSION_CONTEXT.
